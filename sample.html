Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What are your operating hours?

+

We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sundays from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

How do I book an appointment?

+

You can book an appointment by calling us at 0114 464 941, using the booking form on this website, or messaging us on WhatsApp. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you accept walk-ins?

+

Yes, we accommodate walk-ins based on availability. However, we recommend booking in advance to guarantee your preferred time slot.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, M-Pesa, debit/credit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due at the time of service.

How long do services typically take?

+

Haircuts take 30-45 minutes, while spa services like Moroccan baths and massages take 60-90 minutes. We'll provide a time estimate when you book.
